Biography

Wanruo Shi is a PhD Candidate in the School of Education, Tsinghua University. She received her Master of Education (MPhil in Educational Technology) from Tsinghua University (Beijing, China) in 2022. Her research interests include secure control systems, networked control systems and learning to control, with applications in sensor networks and robotics.
